FINANCE, LAW AND TECHNOLOGY

Workshop on "integrated digital transformation systems for enhancing performance higher education institutions "

The Goal Of The Conference

The international conference on finance, law, and technology (icflt 2021), organized By the university of Salamanca it will be held on 6-7 may 2021 (thursday/friday) online The conference aims to bring together academics and researchers to share knowledge And experts to interact with international participants.

Scope

  • Finance banking, green finance economices insurance, Covernance, risk management, insurance administration, Financial instability, banking regulation, Ethics, financial regulation
  • Law corporate social responsibility and legislation in SME. the increase of coronavirus-related crimes. Insolvency law in times of covid-19, cyber security law. Globalization and regulatory change
  • Technology: Digital transformation, financial technology, big-data 4-0, renewable energy, information technology, information system, digital transformation, digital and new professions for tomorrow, green technological innovation, communicatin, E-learning.
