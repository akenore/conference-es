Conference 2022

Finance, Accounting and Law in the Digital Age

Dear colleagues, We are pleased to announce that our Second International Conference will be held on 24-25 November 2022 in Salamanca-Spain. The theme of the conference is 'Finance, Accounting and Law in the Digital Age'. Several high-profile academics, practitioners, and policymakers have been invited to the conference as keynote speakers and panelists. We are inviting you to submit papers under different sub-topics of Finance, Accounting, Law, and Technology. In consultation with the Editors-in-Chief, authors of top conference papers will be invited to submit their papers to be published in an indexed publication. Proposals will clearly and succinctly introduce the topic in no more than 500 words, and should be submitted online on this link before October 15
